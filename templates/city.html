Best Time to Visit

A quick seasonal guide before you pack your bags

Winter

Oct – Feb

Cool, clear days and the most comfortable time for sightseeing and long walks outdoors.

☀️

Summer

Mar – Jun

Warmer afternoons — plan indoor visits and markets midday, and save outdoor spots for morning or evening.

🌧

Monsoon

Jul – Sep

Lush, green landscapes and fewer crowds, though a light raincoat and flexible plans help.

Travel Tips

  • Book hotels and rentals a few days in advance during weekends and holiday seasons.
  • Carry a valid ID — most rental counters ask for one as a deposit or proof.
  • Confirm fuel policy and helmet availability before riding off with a bike or scooter.
  • Keep some cash handy; smaller vendors near attractions may not accept cards.
